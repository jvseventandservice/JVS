Discover the Event Management Blogs with Critical Industry Insights Techsytalk

Liz King Caruso is a tech-savvy planner who knows that integrating technology into your events can drive success. Since Liz was always following technology trends herself, she decided to start an event planning blog to help others discover the latest in event planning technology. Liz King also hosts an annual live event in New York called Techsytalk LIVE, where planners can engage with one another in a tech-forward environment.

Endless Events is a nationwide event production company. They have experience helping their clients put on some pretty memorable events like Comic-Con, The Color Run, and the X-Games. Putting on these events has been no easy task. But the team at Endless is eager to share their events best practices via their event planning blog. Bonus: the Endless blog showcases a weekly, interactive video podcast called #EventIcons. Here, they bring in event industry veterans to do educational interviews for anyone interested in learning more about what makes events successful.

BizBash Though it’s not technically a “blog,” BizBash is still a valuable online resource for all event planners. This site covers event industry news, ideas, and trends. You’ll find coverage of a wide variety of international events. While BizBash’s content will help you learn about the industry, some of their tools can help you in your day-to-day work. Their venue-finding tool and supplier directory can help you to find the right venues and suppliers for any kind of event.

OFD Consulting OFD is a niche marketing and public relations agency that services the wedding industry. But their expertise doesn’t end at just weddings. Meghan Ely, the company’s owner, knows the events industry in and out. Her posts will educate you on topics that cover more than just events, such as advertising, social media, and networking.
